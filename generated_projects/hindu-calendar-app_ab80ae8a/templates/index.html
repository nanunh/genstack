Rahu Kalam

Rahu Kalam is considered an inauspicious time of the day according to Hindu astrology. It is believed that starting new activities during this period may face obstacles.

  • Monday: 7:30 AM - 9:00 AM
  • Tuesday: 3:00 PM - 4:30 PM
  • Wednesday: 12:00 PM - 1:30 PM
  • Thursday: 1:30 PM - 3:00 PM
  • Friday: 10:30 AM - 12:00 PM
  • Saturday: 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM
  • Sunday: 4:30 PM - 6:00 PM

Yamakandam

Yamakandam is another inauspicious period according to Hindu astrology. Like Rahu Kalam, it's advised to avoid starting important activities during this time.

  • Monday: 10:30 AM - 12:00 PM
  • Tuesday: 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM
  • Wednesday: 7:30 AM - 9:00 AM
  • Thursday: 6:00 AM - 7:30 AM
  • Friday: 3:00 PM - 4:30 PM
  • Saturday: 1:30 PM - 3:00 PM
  • Sunday: 12:00 PM - 1:30 PM
